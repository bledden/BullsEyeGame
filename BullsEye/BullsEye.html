~~~~[ Bull's Eye ]~~~~

The goal of the game is to drag the slider as close as possible to the target value that the system picks randomly between 0 and 100. You earn points based on the difference between the target value and where you landed and 100. If you get it exactly, you earn 100 bonus points. Within 1 spot, you earn 50 bonus points. If you get it over 10 spots off, you lose the amount of points you could have earned being under 10 spots away.

Enjoy!
